Writer · Director · Editor

Short film created for a drama production class during the final semester — end-to-end production (including location hunting, talent scouting, styling, and handmade costume design). Lighting & Creative Assistance

조명 & 크리에이티브 어시스턴트

The story follows a young woman struggling after the death of her sister. She breaks up with her boyfriend and is hunted by the drug boss responsible for her sister’s death. Campaign Proposal캠페인 기획안

Instagram Green Ver. Campaign TEAM

인스타그램 그린 버전 캠페인 TEAM

Research · Customisation Tools Planning & Visualisation

리서치 · 커스터마이제이션 도구 기획 및 시각화

Problem: Instagram's engagement-driven design encourages addictive behaviours, social comparison, and reduced focus on real-life relationships.
Solution: Designed "Green Instagram," an ethical social media concept using customisable limits, reduced social pressure cues, and awareness-driven features.
Result: A user experience framework that encourages mindful usage, strengthens authentic communication, and positions the platform as a socially responsible, ESG-aligned brand.

문제: 인스타그램의 참여 중심 설계가 중독적 행동, 사회적 비교, 오프라인 관계 약화를 유발.
솔루션: "그린 인스타그램" — 맞춤형 이용 제한, 사회적 압박 요소 최소화, 인식 기반 기능으로 건강한 사용을 촉진하는 윤리적 SNS 컨셉.
결과: 마음챙김 이용을 장려하고 ESG 브랜드 이미지를 강화하는 사용자 경험 프레임워크.
